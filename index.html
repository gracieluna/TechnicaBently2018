About us:

We are a humane shelter,and we make sure the animals under our care are happy and healthy. We believe in no harsh cages, but instead large enclosures, and 'playtime' for the different animal groups. If you have any further questions,contact us at bentlyadoptionshelter@gmail.com, or 1800BENTLY

Requirments:

There are individual requirements for adopting different types of animals, but all owners must have a place to live. You also must have a feasible budget of about $100 a month for food and a bed. If possible, $25 for toys and also aditional $75 for medicine and other medical costs. So in total, about $200
